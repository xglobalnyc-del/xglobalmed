About X Global

A Bridge Between Innovation and Impact

X Global Inc. is a U.S.-based MedTech consulting and distribution company headquartered in New York. We are dedicated to supporting innovative medical device manufacturers worldwide in achieving successful entry into the U.S. healthcare market.

Through a comprehensive service system integrating regulatory strategy, clinical evaluation, commercialization, and investment partnership, X Global bridges medical innovation and patient impact. We have collaborated with multiple medical technology innovators from Asia and Europe, and built partnerships with U.S. hospitals, physicians, and distributors.
